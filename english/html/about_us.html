About Krasnograd Liceum 2

Date of foundation
September 1, 1944
Director
Shokodko Ivan Hryhorovych, higher education, specialty teacher of history and social sciences, has the highest qualification category. He has been the head of the institution since 2010
Contingent of students
The lyceum has 505 students, including 194 students in grades 1-4, 243 students in grades 5-9, and 68 students in grades 10-11. Graduates of 9 classes - 48 students Graduates of 11th grade - 24 students, including: applicants for the gold medal 2 students. 22 students of the 11th grade took part in the External Evaluation 2020/2021 academic year.
Language of education
Ukrainian
Profile education
The educational process is carried out in the following profile subjects: "Ukrainian language", "Ukrainian literature" (22 scholars); "History of Ukraine", "World History" (46 students)
Teaching staff
The teaching staff consists of 49 teachers, 44 of them have a complete higher education, 2 - incomplete higher education, 3 - basic higher education, 2 - studying with ZVO. 26 teachers have the qualification category "specialist of the highest category", "specialist of the first category" - 8, "specialist of the second category" - 2, "specialist" - 13.
Catering
100% (216 students) are provided with hot free meals for primary school students and children of privileged categories, 370 students consume buffet products.

circles
The lyceum has 5 clubs and a studio "Robotics", which work on a free basis. On the basis of the institution there are clubs of the district center of out-of-school education and the district center of children's and youth creativity, sections of the children's and youth sports school, which are attended by 30% of the children of the institution.
Transportation of students and teachers
67 students from the village of Pokrovsky and the village of Pishchanky are being transported. Transportation is by school bus.
Logistics
There are 19 classrooms and 3 classrooms, two educational and computer complexes (22 PCs), a sports hall, an assembly hall, gymnastics and football fields, a workshop and a dining room. The library fund has - 16911 copies of fiction and educational literature
