The work of the teaching staff

The most important
New dates of winter holidays: 25.12.2021 - 09.01.2022
Distance education
From November 25, in accordance with the order of the Krasnograd City Council Krasnograd Lyceum № 2 will move to distance learning. Lessons are held according to the current schedule for the 2021/2022 school year in synchronous and asynchronous modes. The schedule of synchronous and asynchronous lessons is posted in Viber class groups before classes and is duplicated on the page of the site "Study material for processing".
autumn holidays
In accordance with the order of the Krasnograd City Council, the terms of the autumn holidays have been changed. New dates of autumn holidays 18.10.21 - 22.10.21. October 23, 2021 - a working day for teachers and students (for 15.10.2021). The establishment operates remotely on a Friday schedule. Lessons are conducted synchronously, the training material is posted on the page "Training material for processing".
about bullying
On October 1, a practical psychologist with teachers of the institution - a workshop "School Bullying", which was held by a seminar of teachers with the concepts of "bullying", "school bullying", "cyber-bullying". Teachers supplemented their theoretical knowledge on this topic, learned to respond properly to situations of violence, and finally developed a system of actions of the teaching staff in the event of bullying in the school.
